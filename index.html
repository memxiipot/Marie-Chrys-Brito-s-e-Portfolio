Reflections on College Life

College has been the biggest challenge in my life, thus far. I have been through 2 different courses prior to my current course. I am grateful that I have finally found my path through Information Systems. When I was a culinary arts student in my first year in college, I felt that I should pursue some other course in college then proceed to study the culinary arts after graduating some other bachelor’s degree.

After contemplating on my thought to transfer course, I made the difficult decision to leave my comfort zone and transfer schools to pursue International Studies majoring in European Studies. Sadly this path was not the right choice for me. I was in a difficult situation of trying to fit in a new, bigger school and trying to make new friends while trying to understand the course that I took.

International Studies was not a course that I chose when I tried to enter DLSU. My first choice was Computer Science, followed by Psychology. I passed as a psychology student but then I was informed that there were no more slots available for that course therefore I was forced to choose some other course on the spot. I was intimidated by the course, International Studies, and was later on frustrated with myself. In the later part of my year in Lasalle, I felt discouraged to do well in my studies and consequently had to transfer schools.

When I transferred back to Benilde, I knew I was right at home. I am fascinated to learn about computer languages and designing web and systems therefore I decided that this was the perfect fit for me. I have since been happy with my decision and I am ecstatic to finally graduate after the many years of making life changing mistakes.
